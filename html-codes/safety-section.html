Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our safety features.

How do SOS alerts work?

expand_more

Our one-tap SOS button immediately alerts local emergency services and our 24/7 Response Center. Your precise GPS coordinates and medical profile are shared instantly with verified first responders.

Are all guides background checked?

expand_more

Yes, every guide on SmartTrip CI undergoes a rigorous multi-step verification process, including criminal background checks, identity verification, and professional certification by the Ministry of Tourism.

Is my payment secure?

expand_more

We use bank-grade SSL encryption for all transactions. Our platform never stores your full card details, and we partner with the most trusted local and international payment processors.
